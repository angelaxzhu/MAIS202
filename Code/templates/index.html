Background

Bacteria are pretty cool, but not when they are antibiotic resistant. To help doctors figure out what antibiotics to prescribe, we can analyze the DNA sequence of the bacteria and determine which antibiotics they are immune against. Give it a try by copy-pasting the sequence into the search bar!
